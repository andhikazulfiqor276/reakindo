Description Maintenance

AC maintenance is a periodic repair or maintenance activity to maintain the quality of AC engine performance. AC maintenance generally includes:

  • Cleaning (Indoor and Outdoor AC Unit Washing)
  • Check voltage, compressor amperes, check pressure, temperature settings, supply temperature, return temperature and room temperature
