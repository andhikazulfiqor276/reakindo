Mechanical Electrical Plumbing (MEP) Project

Mechanical engineering is essential for successful construction projects, involving planning, design, execution, operation, and maintenance. MEP engineers are responsible for detailed design and careful selection of equipment, ensuring compliance with established norms and laws.

Design Supply and Installation of VRV/VRF System

VRV/VRF system is a multi-split type air conditioner for commercial buildings that uses variable refrigerant flow control to provide customers with the ability to maintain individual zone control in each room and floor of a building.
