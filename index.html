Profile

PT Rubi Eakon Indonesia (PT Reakindo) has established its reputation from years of experience in the Mechanical, Electrical, Ventilation and Air Conditioning services and are recognised for providing quality, efficient and professional services to commercial, industrial and the resource sector. Design and drafting, manufacture and supply, installation and commissioning provide a quality partner for your M/E & HVAC projects, service and maintenance. PT Reakindo diverse skill set comprises commissioning technicians, mechanic, electricians, air conditioning and duct installers.
